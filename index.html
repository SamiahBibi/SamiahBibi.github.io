Pasta aglio e Olio

  • Time: 15 min
  • Serves: 6 people
Ingredients Quantity
Spaghetti 320g
Extra virgin oil 60g
Garlic 2 segments
Parsley 30g
Fresh chillies 2

All steps

Step 1:Cook spaghetti in salted boiling water until ready reserve some pasta water and drain.

Step 2: In a pan, heat olive oil and saute garlic until golden.

Step 3:Add red pepper flakes and the drained pasta.

Step 4 Garnish with fresh parsley and serve.

Chicken stir-fry

  • Time: 45 min
  • Serves: 6 people
Ingredients Quantity
Chicken breast 500g
Vegetable oil 2 cups
Garlic 2 segments
sesame oil 1 tbsp
soy sauce 2 tbsp

All steps

Step 1:In a pan, heat sesame oil over medium heat.Add chicken and cook until browned.

Step 2:Add garlic and mixed vegetables stir frying for about 5 minutes

Step 3: Pour in soy sauce and cook for another 2 minutes

Step 4Serve hot over cooked rice.

Chocolate Chip Cookies

  • Time: 15 min
  • Serves: 6 people
Ingredients Quantity
Butter 200g
Brown sugar 150g
White sugar 100 g
Eggs 2
Flour 300g
Chocolate chips 200g
Baking soda 1 tsp

All steps

Step 1:Preheat oven to 180C

Step 2: In a bowl, cream together butter,brown sugar, white sugar.Beat in eggs and vanilla.

Step 3:Combine flour, backing soda, gradually mix in to the wet ingredients. Stir in chocolate chips

Step 4Drop spoonfuls onto a baking sheet and bake for 10-12 minutes.
